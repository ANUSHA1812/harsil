Detailed Itinerary:

Day 1:

Arrive at your pickup location and our representative will welcome you. Drive to Harshil. Check into the Hotel and after that proceed, start your day with scrumptious breakfast and make your way to Harsil, an unspoiled gem of Uttarakhand on the bank of Bhagirathi River. Your journey to this beautiful hamlet would take approximately 7 hours and you will pass by famous Dharasu Bend, Uttarkashi, and Uttarkashi Vishwanath temple. Make your long journey a relaxing one by stopping at Gangnani to take a dip in the hot spring. Enjoy a delicious local lunch at  Uttarkashi and refuel your systems for the journey ahead. You will receive a traditional welcome at Harsil and check-in at the Chardham camp. After a short tea break, find your way to Mukhwas Village, a popular tourist’s attraction that is hardly a kilometer away from Harsil. Mukhwas Village is mainly known for being the winter abode of Goddess Gangotri during snowfall, making the place pure and beautiful. People visit here in large numbers to worship the Goddess and admire the beauty of the place. Indulge in a hot cup of tea and then meet the experts who would tell you more about the temple and the goddess. After your tour, head on for dinner and then return to your room for a comfortable overnight stay.

Day 2:

Start your day by undertaking a nature walk to Wilson House, 1.5 kilometers from the camp. It is the prominent house of Camp- E. Wilson who introduced the villagers of Tehri to the practice of apple cultivation and also built a suspension bridge over Jadganga for local people. Currently, there's no bridge but one could still see its traces. Having explored the place, head back to the hotel and embark on a 22 kilometers drive to reach the  Gangotri Dham, dedicated to Goddess Ganga. The sacred Ganga originates at Gaumukh from the Gangotri glacier, which is 18 km from the town of Gangotri. Take a dip in the holy Ganga, also called Bhagirathi at its origin. Visit the temple to see the small silver idol of Mother Ganga. Perform pooja and relax in the lovely surrounding. Don't forget to click pictures of the amazing mountain range of the Himalayas and the flowing Bhagirathi. In the afternoon, return back to Harsil for lunch. You will be welcomed back to camp with a cup of hot tea and proceed to enjoy dinner while listening to the soothing Katha Saar in the background. After dinner drive to Delhi.

About the Place:

Harshil is a village and a cantonment area on the banks of the  Bhagirathi River , on the way to the  Hindu pilgrimage, Gangotri, in  Uttarkashi district of the Indian state of  Uttarakhand ). Situated at a height of 7,860 ft (2,620 metres.) from sea level, Harshil lies 73 km. from  Uttarkashi , and 30 km away from Harshil lies the Gangotri National Park, spread over 1,553 square km.

Overview

Come on an amazing 5-day trip to three magnificent cities of India - Delhi, Jaipur and Agra. Delhi, India's capital, is famous for relics of lost empires, colourful markets and lip-smacking food. Visit the enthralling historic city of Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan. The major attractions here are the City Palace and Hawa Mahal. Head to Agra and visit the splendid Taj Mahal. Come visit the wonderful states of North India and unravel the deep mysteries and legends that still haunt these places. The spectacular mix of people, traditions and landscapes will blaze in your memory for a long time.
